[resinterests]Research</b>

My PhD thesis dealt with the [interests]Pittburgh model of Learning Classifier Systems (LCS)[/interests]. Specifically, the thesis had the following objectives:

  * [interests]Improving the generalization capacity of the model[/interests]

  * [interests]Reducing the run-time of the system[/interests]

  * [interests]Proposing rule-based representations for real-valued attributes[/interests]

You can download my thesis here


Currently I work on the [interests]bioinformatics field[/interests], [interests]applying LCSs to protein structure prediction[/interests], in a project called [interests]Robust Prediction[/interests] with Explanatory Power for Protein Structure and [interests]Related Prediction Problems[/interests] under the supervision of Dr. Natalio Krasnogor[/resinterests]
