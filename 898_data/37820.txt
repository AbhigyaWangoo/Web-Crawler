My current research interests are in[interests] audio and video editing[/interests], [interests]indexing[/interests], and [interests]retrieval[/interests]. I worked at Xerox PARC for 10 years on various projects involving audio and image wordspotting and segmentation. Before PARC, I worked at Hewlett Packard Laboratories developing a speech recognition system for personal computers. I have twenty-two patents in audio and image technologies, and have published a number of papers. I have a Ph.D. in [phdmajor]Mathematical Sciences[/phdmajor] from [phduniv]Rice University[/phduniv].[/introduction]
